A city with a profound history !

Nantes' historical nickname was "Venice of the West" (French: la Venise de l'Ouest), a reference to the many quays and river channels in the old town before they were filled in during the 1920s and 1930s. The elephant is not the only emblem of Nantes. The city is best known for its football team and passionate supporters. The stadium, named "La Beaujoire" has been described many times as the best atmosphere of the whole championship.
